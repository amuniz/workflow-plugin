Milestone Ordinal.

It is a possitive number which serves two purposes:

  1. Unambiguously identify the milestone in the scope of the script
  2. Determine the ordering of the milestone respect other milestones in the script

As this step forces builds to pass through it in-order it requires an ordering field. The value assigned to this field needs to comply with the following: whichever two consecutive milestones their ordinals must be two consecutive natural numbers.
